About Us

The National Honor Society is a nationwide organization for high school students in the United States who have shown several distinguishing qualities. The Collegium Charter School NHS chapter selects its members based on the four pillars of NHS: scholarship, leadership, service, and character. Members of the National Honor Society work together to organize many events that benefit their community. Members are also expected to maintain the four requirements of NHS and perform a set number of community service hours as well as an independent service project each year.

Mission Statement

The purpose of this organization is to recognize and create an enthusiasm for scholarship, to stimulate a desire to render service, to promote worthy leadership, and to encourage the development of character in the students of Collegium Charter High School.
