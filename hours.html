How to Submit Hours

  1. Go to Naviance.com and sign in to your Naviance account.
  2. Use your school email to log in. Your password is likely your first and last initials and your lunch PIN. If you cannot log in, contact your counselor to get help resetting your password.
  3. Click on the link to x2VOL under "Resources," which should be either at the bottom of the page or on the left hand side.
  4. Hover your mouse over Groups & Goals on the top of the page and click on My Group & Goals.
  5. Click on the green Add New Group button on the right side on the page.
  6. Click the green Join button next to National Honor Society.
  7. Now when you submit hours on x2VOL, you can choose to apply them to your Independent Service Project (ISP), or your NHS service hours.
