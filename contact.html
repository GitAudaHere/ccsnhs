The following are the CCS National Honor Society officers of the 2018-2019 school year. The officers work together with the advisor to organize many events for the community throughout the year.

Mrs. Brenda Fronheiser

Advisor

Email: bfronheiser@ccs.us

The advisor serves as an adult leader to guide the society, maintain files on the chapter, review members for compliance with the by-laws, and oversee the general management of the chapter.

Abigail Dean

President

Email: abigail.dean@ccs.us

The president oversees the other officers and members of the chapter, chairs all meetings, and acts as a spokesperson for the entire chapter.

Zachary Essick

Vice-President

Email: zachary.essick@ccs.us

The vice-president fills in for the president when needed and shares the duties and responsibilities of the president.

Marshall Lentz

Secretary

Email: marshall.lentz@ccs.us

The secretary keeps detailed records of meetings and service hours, and updates the president and vice-president on a monthly basis.

Cheney Ni

Treasurer

Email: cheney.ni@ccs.us

The treasurer oversees all fundraising activities, keep detailed records of revenue and spending, and proposes a budget to be voted upon at the beginning of the school year.

Mina Bourquin

Public Relations

Email: mina.bourquin@ccs.us

The public relations officer notifies members and students of upcoming NHS events, fundraisers, and activities through the use of school announcements, emails, and newsletters.

Kendra Ebke

Historian

Email: kendra.ebke@ccs.us

The historian documents all activities of the NHS through the use of written accounts and photographic documentation, and oversees the creation and distribution of the induction invitations and programs.
