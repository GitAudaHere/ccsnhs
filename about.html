About Us

The National Honor Society is a nationwide organization for high school students in the United States who have shown several distinguishing qualities. The Collegium Charter School NHS chapter selects its members based on the four pillars of NHS: scholarship, leadership, service, and character. Members of the National Honor Society work together to organize many events that benefit their community. NHS members are expected to maintain the four requirements of NHS and perform set number of community service hours as well as a independent service project.
