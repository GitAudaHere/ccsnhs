2018-2019 Events

Meet the Teacher Night (August 15)

Meet the Teacher Night is a chance for upcoming students to meet with their teachers for the new school year, find their classes and rooms in a pressure-free setting, and compare schedules with their friends. It's always important to familiarize yourself with the school before showing up on the first day!

School Supply Drive (August 27-31)

The School Supply Drive is a great way for students and their families to donate supplies such as pencils, notebooks, tissues, and other classroom necessities to families in need. To donate, simply purchase school supplies and put them in a "School Supply Drive" bin, located at the front office of each building.

Back to School Night (September 5-8)

Back to School Night is a great way for parents to learn a little bit about their child's school day and meet with their teachers to discuss curriculum details and coursework.

Fall Bake Sale (October 3)

Come support the NHS by purchasing baked goods at our fall bake sale! Available from 5th to 8th period outside the 535 cafeteria.

Fall Blood Drive (November 9)

There are always people in need of a lifesaving blood transfusion. Remember to sign up to donate if you can and encourage others to do the same! Find out if you are eligible to donate.

NHS Induction (November 15)

Every year we welcome several new members to our chapter of the National Honor Society. These inductees have proven themselves to be of good character, possessed strong leadership and scholarship skills, and dedicated themselves in service to their community. We hope that you will come and support our new National Honor Society members.

Decorating Nursing Homes (December 1)

Share the Christmas joy by helping us decorate nursing homes for the holidays! Permission slips available here.

Taking Down Decorations (January 12)

What goes up must come down! Help us remove these decorations from the nursing homes so that they can make way for the next event.

Heifer Fund (Panera) (January 28 - February 1)

Come and support families in need by dining at Panera from January 28th to February 1st! 10% of your purchase will go towards buying a cow for families in need. Make sure to show the cashier this pamphlet so they know you're with us.

Our "Read Across America" competition sees high school students read one book in each of the fifty states of America. Will our students be able to complete Oregon again this year? We'll have to wait and see! In case you haven't noticed, I have no clue what this actually is.

Penny Wars (April 29 - May 3)

The penny wars are back! Face off against other grades as you compete to raise the most money. Pennies are good to put in your jar, but other money counts as negative, so put other coins and dollar bills in your opponents' jars! Whichever class's jar has the highest total at the end of the week wins!

Prom (fashion show) at Nursing Home (May 11)

Who doesn't love a good fashion show? Come and join us as we take a stroll down the runway for the benefit of local nursing homes!
