2018-2019 Events

Meet the Teacher Night (August 15)

Meet the Teacher Night is a chance for upcoming students to meet with their teachers for the new school year, find their classes and rooms in a pressure-free setting, and compare schedules with their friends. It's always important to familiarize yourself with the school before showing up on the first day!

School Supply Drive (August 27-31)

The School Supply Drive is a great way for students and their families to donate supplies such as pencils, notebooks, tissues, and other classroom necessities to families in need. To donate, simply purchase school supplies and put them in a "School Supply Drive" bin, located at the front office of each building.

Back to School Night (September 5-8)

Back to School Night is a great way for parents to learn a little bit about their child's school day and meet with their teachers to discuss curriculum details and coursework.

Fall Bake Sale (October 3)

Come support the NHS by purchasing baked goods at our fall bake sale! Available from 5th to 8th period outside the 535 cafeteria.

Fall Blood Drive (November 9)

There are always people in need of a lifesaving blood transfusion. Remember to sign up to donate if you can and encourage others to do the same!
